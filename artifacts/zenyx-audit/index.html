Three Things That Can
Shut Down Your Hospital

These are the three IT failure modes that cause the most damage — and most hospitals don't know their exposure.

Ransomware & Data Loss

Attackers encrypt patient records, HIS/EMR systems, and PACS — and demand payment to unlock them. Hospitals with no tested backup have no choice but to pay.

Avg recovery: 3–7 days downtime + data loss

System & Network Downtime

An unplanned server failure or network outage stops admissions, diagnostics, and billing simultaneously. Staff resort to paper — creating delays and clinical errors.

₹80K–₹5L+ revenue lost per hour of downtime

Unauthorized Data Access

Patient data accessed by former employees, shared Wi-Fi, or misconfigured systems. DPDP Act 2023 fines can reach ₹250 crore for healthcare organizations.

DPDP Act penalties + reputational damage
